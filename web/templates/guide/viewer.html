Getting Started

Welcome to the IPN Southeast Events Portal. As a Viewer, you have read-only access to all approved events and planning views.

{{/* Step 1 */}}
1

Sign In

Use the link from your welcome email to set your password.

→ Click Set Password & Sign In in your email

→ Choose a password (minimum 8 characters)

→ You'll be signed in automatically

→ Next time: use Login with Email on the portal

{{/* Step 2 */}}
2

Calendar View

See all approved events laid out month by month.

→ Click Calendar in the top navigation

→ Events with confirmed dates appear on the calendar grid

→ Recurring events show as color-coded bars across their active months

→ Click any event name to view its full details

{{/* Step 3 */}}
3

Roadmap View

All approved events organized by quarter.

→ Click Roadmap in the top navigation

→ Events are grouped into Q1–Q4 with a color-coded card for each

→ Each card shows the event name, description, and assigned date

→ Use Download PDF to export the full roadmap as a PDF

{{/* Step 4 */}}
4

Event Details

Dig into the full details of any event.

→ Click any event card or name to open its detail page

→ You can see the full event brief: inputs, activities, outcomes, support requests

→ Viewing is read-only — reach out to an admin to make changes

Go to the Portal →
